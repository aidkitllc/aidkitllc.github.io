Grand Lake Relief Fund


Applicant Agreement


As an applicant of the Grand Lake Relief Fund, I understand my expectations to be as follows:


Applicant Attestations: I attest that the following statements are true:

  • The information and any documentation I shall supply in the grant screening process will be accurate, true and complete

  • I am a business owner in Grand Lake, CO

  • I am in need of cash assistance due to financial hardship as a result of the East Troublesome Fire and

    COVID-19 pandemic


    Providing Documentation: To provide the required documentation to the best of my ability. Required documentation for assistance includes the following:

  • Valid business license from the State of Colorado

  • Proof of business revenue (proof can include a profit and loss statement, bank statement with deposit record, or contact details of a trusted reference)

  • Proof of business operation of at least 20 hrs/week (proof online postings including hours of

    operation, or contact details of a trusted reference)

  • Proof of necessary bills required for payment (rent, utility, inventory)


I have read and understand the information on this contract.


Signed
